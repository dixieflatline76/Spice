Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Spice Wallpaper Manager and the Spice Wallpaper Manager Extension handle your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data remains under your control.

Data Collection and Usage

  • No Personal Identificable Information (PII): We do not collect names, emails, or addresses.
  • Local-Only Operation: Spice is a 100% client-side application. No central servers are used to store your data.
  • Google Photos: We only access the photos you explicitly select via the Google Photos Picker. References are stored strictly on your local machine.

No Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties. All your configurations stay on your personal device.

Open Source Transparency

Spice is open-source. You can review our full source code and build logs on GitHub to verify our security and privacy claims.
